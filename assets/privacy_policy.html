Privacy Policy for Smart Safe School

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Smart Safe School ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). This policy applies to all users of the Smart Safe School application, including students, teachers, and administrators.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect the following types of personal information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, user type (student/teacher/admin)
  • Student Information: Student ID, class assignments, gender, parent/guardian contact information
  • Teacher Information: Teacher ID, subject assignments, class assignments
  • Administrator Information: Admin ID, school management permissions
  • Authentication Data: Login credentials, session tokens

2.2 Academic Data

  • Attendance Records: Daily attendance status, absence records, attendance dates
  • Grade Information: Academic grades, test scores, semester performance
  • Academic Progress: Subject performance, academic year data

2.3 Document and File Data

  • Document Uploads: Files uploaded by teachers and administrators
  • Document Metadata: File names, upload dates, document descriptions
  • Document Interactions: Read status, favorite status, download history

2.4 Technical Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version
  • App Usage Data: Features used, session duration, error logs
  • Network Information: IP address, connection type

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Educational Services: Providing core school management functionality
  • User Authentication: Verifying user identity and managing access
  • Academic Tracking: Recording and managing student attendance and grades
  • Communication: Facilitating document sharing between teachers, students, and administrators
  • App Improvement: Analyzing usage patterns to improve app functionality
  • Security: Protecting against unauthorized access and maintaining data integrity
  • Compliance: Meeting educational and legal requirements

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 Within the School System

Information is shared within the school system as follows:

  • Teachers can access information about their assigned students and classes
  • Students can access their own academic records and documents
  • Administrators can access information necessary for school management
  • Parents/guardians may access their child's information through authorized channels

4.2 Third-Party Service Providers

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database hosting and user authentication services
  • Cloud Storage: Secure file storage for documents and images

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law, court order, or government regulation.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Role-based access restrictions
  • Authentication: Secure login systems with password protection
  • Regular Updates: Security patches and system updates
  • Monitoring: Continuous monitoring for security threats

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Account Information: Retained while account is active
  • Academic Records: Retained according to educational record-keeping requirements
  • Document Files: Retained for the duration of the academic relationship
  • Technical Logs: Retained for security and troubleshooting purposes (typically 90 days)

7. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request access to your personal information
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Portability: Request a copy of your information in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to certain processing of your information

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for educational use and may be used by minors. We comply with applicable children's privacy laws:

  • We collect information from students only for educational purposes
  • Parent/guardian consent is obtained where required by law
  • We do not use student information for advertising or marketing
  • Student information is not sold or shared for commercial purposes

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through the app or other appropriate means.
